About Bees

Bees are essential pollinators, playing a critical role in our ecosystems and agriculture.

There are over 20,000 species of bees worldwide, each with unique characteristics and roles.

Importance of Bees

Bees are vital for pollinating many of the crops we rely on for food. Without bees, our food supply would be greatly diminished.

They also contribute to the biodiversity of plants and help maintain the balance of our natural environments.
