Welcome to the World of Bees

Discover the amazing life of bees and the important role they play in our world. Bees play a vital role in maintaining biodiversity and supporting agriculture through pollination. With over 20,000 species worldwide, bees are responsible for pollinating a significant portion of the crops we eat, as well as wild plants. These hardworking insects live in colonies with a structured society, where each bee has a specific role. Their survival is crucial for food production and the health of ecosystems, making them essential to our planet's future.
